French Crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Kitchen Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Whisk
  • 8" skillet
  • Stove
  • Ladle

Directions

  • Mix water and milk in bowl
  • whisk eggs into the milk and water mix
  • slowly add flour until all gone and smooth consitancy
  • add salt and oil
  • heat skillet on stove at medium heat
  • Ladle batter into pan tiliting batter to cover entire pan base
  • flip when edges curl away from pan
  • remove when golden brown repeat until batter is gone
  • Serve with sugar, lemon, butter, and salt
